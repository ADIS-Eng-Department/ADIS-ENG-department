Department of English

Our Vision

The Department is committed to ensure that all students make outstanding progress in the four skill areas of the subject, foster greater independence in their work, a love for language and literature as well as facilitate and engage students in activities that enhance their confidence in the use of the language. Also, to educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society.

Our Mission

The Department promotes literacy, specifically the ability to read, write, speak and think critically. We aim to promote higher order language skills and prepare for entry into the professional domain. The Department aspires to provide activities requiring hands-on experience as well as opportunities for contemplation and discussion. Also, to aim for systematic reform and continuous improvement.
